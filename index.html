About Me

Hi there! I'm Matt, a front-end developer based in Montreal and I'm passionate about creating beautiful and accessible websites. My background is in classical trombone, and through my career in classical music I have extensive experience collaborating with colleagues to create professional, unified musical products for concert performances. The years of daily practice that being a musician entails have led me to be extremely patient with incremental progress and methodical in my approach to creative problem-solving. Beyond coding and practicing trombone, I love going on long runs and listening to overly analytical podcasts about the NBA.
